## About this file

Paste or write your product documentation here. Recommended sections:

- **Product overview** — what the product does and who it is for
- **Core features** — key capabilities with clear, factual descriptions
- **Modules / add-ons** — any optional components and what they include
- **Supported platforms / integrations** — OS, cloud, third-party compatibility
- **Reports and dashboards** — report names, counts, categories
- **Alerts** — alert names and triggering conditions
- **Compliance coverage** — which standards the product supports and how
- **Deployment** — how the product is installed, licensed, and maintained
