| email address | email ID / mail ID |

## Voice and tone

- **Second person** throughout: you, your, you can, your team
- **Active voice** as default
- **Direct, declarative** sentence openers — no "In this section, we will..."
- **No hedging** — write "X detects Y" not "X can potentially detect Y"
- **No filler intros** — do not open paragraphs with "In today's landscape..."

## Formatting rules

- **Sentence case** for all headings (H1, H2, H3)
- **Oxford comma** in all lists
- **Bold** for product feature names on first mention in a section
- **No em dashes** (—) in body copy — use a comma, semicolon, or parentheses

## Numbers

- Spell out one through nine in body prose
- Use numerals for 10 and above
- Use numerals for percentages and statistics (e.g., 300+ reports, 25% reduction)
