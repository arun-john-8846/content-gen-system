# SERP and PAA research instructions

> **PLACEHOLDER — replace with your specific research workflow.**
>
> The pipeline loads this file when running SERP research.
> Tailor these instructions to your product category and target audience.

---

## Research goals

For each seed keyword, the research phase collects:

1. **AI Overview / featured snippet** — what Google surfaces at position 0
2. **People Also Ask (PAA)** — live questions users are asking (use for FAQ)
3. **Top 10 organic results** — competitor pages and their angles
4. **SERP feature analysis** — are there tables, lists, knowledge panels?

---

## Step 1: US VPN required

All searches must be run with a US VPN active. SERP results vary by region.

## Step 2: Collect AI Overview

- Search the exact seed keyword
- Copy the full AI Overview text if present
- Note which sources it cites

## Step 3: Collect PAA questions

- Expand all PAA boxes for the seed keyword
- Record 8–10 questions verbatim
- Use the 5–6 most relevant as FAQ questions in the draft

## Step 4: Analyse top 10 results

For each of the top 10 organic results, note:

- Page title and URL
- Primary angle (what problem does this page solve?)
- Word count (estimate)
- Headings used (H1, H2)
- Content gaps — what does this page NOT cover?

## Step 5: Identify gaps and owned angles

Based on the SERP analysis:

- List 3–5 topics the top results cover well (table stakes — must include)
- List 2–3 topics the top results ignore or cover poorly (differentiation opportunity)
- Identify the product-specific angle that no competitor can replicate

---
